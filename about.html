Our Story and Where it all began


Thoseens Rentals started as a small idea in a family garage in Milton. It began with just a few tables, chairs, and a deep belief that everyone deserves access to beautiful, affordable event décor — no matter the occasion.


Our founder, inspired by the lack of accessible rental options in her community, started offering décor and furniture for friends' gatherings and local events. Word quickly spread, and what was once a weekend side hustle turned into a full-service rental business trusted across the GTA.

Today, we continue to grow — fueled by passion, community support, and a love for helping people create unforgettable moments.
From weddings and backyard parties to corporate events, Thoseens is here to bring your vision to life.
